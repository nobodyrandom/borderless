GET INVOLVED

BORDERLESS is looking for talented and passionate volunteers. Get involved in one of our branches today

Logistics Branch


The Logistics Branch assures that every event is executed flawlessly. Responsibilities range from booking venues to organizing a catering service, as well as managing technical issues during events. Developing shifts for the team throughout the event, as well as planning new meeting spaces and times is also a crucial part of this branch. The Logistics Branch also includes brainstorming venues and ideas for casual team outings and get-togethers. Last but not least, this branch seeks unique items for our auctioning fundraisers. Strong attention to detail is an asset.


Logistics Coordinator

Toronto ON
Job Description

Logistics Coordinators work in the Logistics Branch to plan and organize events in a variety of ways. They assist the branch by outlining the details of various club functions, then follow through with the completion of needed tasks. This could include searching for and booking venues, acquiring event supplies, planning BORDERLESS socials, and working shifts at our events. Logistics Coordinators are essential in effectively carrying out not only the planning but also the execution of the clubs' functions.

Marketing Branch


The Marketing Branch is the face of the organization. It is responsible for maintaining an updated look for BORDERLESS. Graphic designers and web developers develop poster content and manage the group website. Photographers work towards creating visuals to enrich the organization’s public representation. This branch is also responsible for creating a social media strategy that maintains constant presence on BORDERLESS’s Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram accounts. Creating posts with visuals and keeping up with informative responses in a timely manner is important in order to keep our followers engaged at all times. Creative individuals are encouraged to apply.

Financial Branch

This branch is also in charge of managing the group’s budget, looking up the most cost-effective materials, and, most importantly, coming up with new fundraising ideas.

About us

BORDERLESS is a grassroots, non-partisan, non-profit organization which uses creative means of expression to draw attention to, educate on, and raise funds for often overlooked internally displaced persons (IDP) and refugee crises around the world by bringing together people of various backgrounds.

Our long-term vision is to be able to provide medical and general assistance to areas in immediate need. Funds raised by BORDERLESS and other student clubs, societies, and organizations would be sent to regions with large numbers of refugees that do not receive sufficient aid.
